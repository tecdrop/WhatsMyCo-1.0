How does it work?

WhatsMyCo computes the CRC-32 (cyclic redundancy check) of any text you are typing, on the fly. That's a 32 bit number that is instantly converted to a RGBA color code, with transparency.
(Your current CRC-32 value is 70255e90)

Why CRC?

CRCs are both popular and famous.
They are commonly used in digital networks and storage devices to detect accidental changes to data. If you CRC the same text more than once, you will get the same CRC every time. If you CRC two different texts, you should get two very different CRCs. We believe this makes it a great choice for turning a text (or file) into a matching color.

Your Privacy

WhatsMyCo is a client web app, written in plain HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. What you type stays in your browser. What files you upload also stay in your browser. Nothing gets uploaded to our servers.
